Save the Amazon

This is a global campaign aiming to protect the Amazon rainforest from deforestation and other threats. Led by environmental organizations, indigenous communities, and activists, the movement seeks to raise awareness about the importance of the Amazon for biodiversity and climate regulation. Through advocacy and action, it aims to pressure governments and corporations to enact policies that preserve the rainforest and respect the rights of indigenous peoples.
